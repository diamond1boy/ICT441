Zhejiang University


Please understand us

Zhejiang University is a national key university organized by the State, The school is located in Hangzhou and its motto is "seeking truth and innovation". supervised by the administrative department of Education under The State Council and co-built by the People's Government of Zhejiang Province. Zhejiang University is a comprehensive, research-oriented and innovative university with distinctive characteristics.


With a total area of 6890108 square meters, it has 13 disciplines, 7 academic departments, 39 professional colleges (departments), 1 engineering college,
2 Chinese-foreign cooperative educational institutions, 7 directly affiliated hospitals, and 9778 faculty members.

Zhejiang University is a national "211 Project", "985 project" and "double first-class" construction university.
As of October 12, 2022, Zhejiang University ranks 67th in The Times Higher Education 2023 World University Rankings.
